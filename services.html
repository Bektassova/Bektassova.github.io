What is a Work Permit in Kazakhstan?

A work permit in Kazakhstan is an official document that allows foreign nationals to legally reside and work in the country. It enables them to enter into employment agreements and pursue jobs in various sectors across Kazakhstan.

How Long Does It Take to Obtain a Work Permit?

The process of obtaining a work permit in Kazakhstan typically takes around one month, provided all necessary documents are submitted on time. Both the employer and employee are responsible for providing the required documentation. The process includes submitting application forms, translating and notarizing personal documents, and completing the C3 visa procedure if necessary. The timeline can vary based on how quickly the employer and employee submit the necessary documents.
